Leaves your host page intact

Extend your current web and landing pages with dialogs or dialog based tutorials without changing your current sites content.

Design dialogs separately

Let your designers create nice looking dialogs without knowing where those will be used later.

Lightweight

Only embed a small amout of Javascript and let the ResponsiveDialog and jQuery mobile integrate dialogs with your site.

UI responsive and touch enabled

It is based on jQuery mobile templates and styles that guarantess dialogs will scale to the dimensons of the site visitors' screens.

Supports dialog flows

With dialogs linked to eachother and/or consecutively you may also create easy page forward and back tutorials.

Open source

It is an open initiative to help creating nice looking dialogs for developers, on both the desktop and mobile web. Learn more, and download from the ResponsiveDialog Github page.
